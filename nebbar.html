Advantage of E-commerce
1.	Fast buying/selling procedure, easy to find product.
2.	Buying/selling 24/7
3.	More reach to customer, there is no theoretical geographic limitation.
4.	Low optional cost and better quality of services.
5.	No need a physical company setup.
6.	Easy to start and manage the business.
7.	Customer can easily select product from different provider without moving around physically.

DisAdvantage
1.	Any one good or bad can easily start a business
2.	There is no guaranty of product quality.
3.	Mechanical failure can cause unpredictable effects on the total processes.
4.	As there is minimum chance of direct customer to company interaction, customer loyalty is always on a check.
5.	 There are many hackers who look for opportunity thus on a e-commerce site, service, payment gateway all
are always prone to attack.

Benefits  of e-commerce:

2)	Digitization of product and processes.

4)	Benefits of e-commerce to society enables more flexible working practices which enhance the quality of life.
Seven unique features of e-commerce.
1)	Ubiquity:
In traditional commerce of market place is restricted i.e. we can be in limited physical area to buy or sell
however e-commerce gives global exposure to buy or sell goods or services.
2)	Global reach:
Unlike traditional commerce, e-commerce technology permits commercial transaction to cross cultural and
national boundaries far more conveniently and cost effectively.
3)	Universal standards.one strikingly unusual feature of e-commerce is that the technical standards of the
internet and therefore the technical standards for conducting e-commerce are universal standards.
4)	Richness : with the use of e-commerce technology merchant can present their message in effective way.
